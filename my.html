Compound Interest Calculator

Welcome to the Compound Interest Calculator!

With this handy tool, you can easily calculate the compound interest on your investments. Whether you're planning for the future or analyzing the growth of your savings, this calculator will provide you with valuable insights.

Simply enter the principal amount, the annual interest rate, the time period (in years), and the compound frequency. Click the "Calculate" button, and let the calculator do the rest. It will compute the compound interest and display the total amount, giving you a clear picture of your investment's growth over time.
